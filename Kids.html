Kid's Classes

We run classes in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Mixed Martial Arts and Muay Thai suitable for children aged between 5 and 10. These classes are touch-contact only and focus on fundamentals in these three disciplines to give participants a solid foundational knowledge, while simultaneously keeping things playful and fun to keep them engaged. All classes are under the careful supervision of one of our instructors.

What equipment do I need for Kid's classes?

The Kid's MMA and Muay Thai classes are touch-contact only, so sparring equipment such as gloves and shin guards is considered unnecessary. If, however, you would still like your child to be protected, we have a limited supply of protective equipment for kids on the premises. They are also welcome to bring their own.
