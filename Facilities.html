Facilities

Matted area

We boast an area of over 500 square metres of matted floors and walls for striking and grappling, spread out over two storeys.


MMA Area

Our MMA Area comes equipped with a large supply of grappling dummies, padded walls and striking pads, in addition to a competition-sized Octagon MMA Cage.


Boxing Area

Our Boxing Area contains a full-sized professional boxing ring and a large assortment of Heavy Bags, Uppercut Bags, Speed Bags, Double-Ended Bags, Skipping Ropes and Focus Pads.


Reception Area

Contains three large sofas and vending machines selling coffee and cold drinks for members to chill out and have a chat.


Fully-Equipped Gym

We house a fully-equipped Irish Strength Institute (ISI) gymnasium complete with Treadmills, Aerodyne Bicycles, Rowing Machines an extensive free-weights area suitable for anything from Bench Pressing to TRX Suspension Training.


Changing Room

Our changing facilities contain two 50 square metre dressing rooms for males and females each containing 10 shower cubicles.


Sports Therapy Area

Our Physical Therapy clinic caters to treatments such as:

  • Sports Massage
  • Soft Tissue Release
  • Muscle Energy Technique
  • Trigger Point Therapy
  • Positional Release
  • Differential Diagnosis
  • Dry Needling.
