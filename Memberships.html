Memberships

  • If you plan on attending at least a couple of classes per week on an ongoing basis, then registering for membership is the way to go.
  • Currently, membership costs €100 per month. All membership payments must be made by direct debit via standing order.
  • If you decide to sign up for a membership, the minimum period that must expire before you are eligible to cancel is 3 months from the date of the signature on your membership contract.
  • If you are unsure of whether you would like to sign up for membership straight away, we recommend that you obtain a Class Pass, detailed below.

Class Pass

  • If you are having doubts about committing to a membership straight away, we advise you to purchase a Class Pass.
  • These passes can be purchased at the Team Impact Reception Area. You can pay for one of these via cash or card.
  • When you buy a Class Pass and intend to use it to attend one of our classes (assuming you satisfy the Eligibility Requirements of that class), simply present it to the instructor and they will mark down one class on your pass before beginning.
  • The table below details all types of Class Passes that we sell and what they entitle the owner to.
Team Impact - Class Passes
Pass Type Price What it entitles the owner to
Single €10 Attend a single class
Day €20 Attend unlimited classes for a single day
5-Class €40 Attend 5 classes
10-class €70 Attend 10 classes

Private Classes

  • Members also have the option of requesting a private class with one of our instructors.
  • All Private classes are currently €30 per hour.

Physio Appointments

  • Members can also avail of an appointment with our in-house Physiotherapist Sean Reilly.
  • All Physio appointments are currently €25 per hour.
