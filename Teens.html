Teens Classes

These follow on from our Kid's Classes and are suitable for individuals between the ages of 11 and 15. Participants in the Teens MMA and Muay Thai classes are allowed to engage in light contact sparring, under the condition that they are closely supervised and wear protection deemed sufficient by the class instructor [see Instructors]. Please see the tables below to view available classes and the required equipment for each.

What equipment do I need for Teens classes?

Please view the table below view required equipment for our Teens classes, taking into account the Gym Rules in relation to this subject. If you wish to do a particular class but do not have the mandatory equipment needed, we have a limited supply on the premises. However, we cannot guarantee you will be able to use it so it is strongly recommended that you bring your own. If you wish to buy some equipment, check out our list of useful websites to get you started.
